# 📊 Sales Overview Dashboard (By Region)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Overview Dashboard** designed to analyze regional performance across multiple years. It provides insights into sales, profit, and quantity trends, enabling quick decision-making through visual analytics.

The dashboard focuses on:

* Regional performance comparison (Central, West, East, South)
* Year-over-Year (YoY) analysis
* State-level sales distribution
* Profitability and quantity tracking

---

## 🎯 Key Features

* **KPI Cards by Region**

  * Total Sales
  * Previous Year Sales
  * Monthly trend visualization

* **Geographical Analysis**

  * Sales distribution across U.S. states
  * Region-based color segmentation

* **Top States Breakdown**

  * Ranked bar chart of high-performing states

* **Year & Date Filters**

  * Toggle between multiple years (2021–2024)
  * Custom date range selection

* **Performance Table**

  * Current vs Previous Year comparison
  * YoY growth (Sales, Profit, Quantity)

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Explained

* **CY Sales**: Current Year Sales
* **PY Sales**: Previous Year Sales
* **YoY Sales %**: Growth compared to previous year
* **Profit**: Net earnings
* **Quantity**: Units sold

## 📈 Buiness Insights

* West leads performance with highest sales and strong growth (+33% YoY), making it the primary revenue driver.
* East shows steady growth (+18% YoY) with solid profit increase—reliable for expansion.
* Central is flat (≈0% growth) with declining profit, indicating cost or pricing issues.
* South is high-risk—sales growing but profit down sharply (-50%), signaling margin problems.
* Overall sales up +20%, but profit growth (+14%) is slower → margin pressure.
*Sales peak in Q4, highlighting strong seasonality.
